Rules : 
	Press the keys A, S, D and F to burn the note.
	Press the key esc to pause the game.
	Multiple keys can be pressed simultaneously. 
	Click on the appopriate key when the note is in the the rectangle at the bottom of the window.
	You can see your score and some other informations on the right panel";
	When you burn a note, if it become green, the note is burn and you win 100 points. 
	if it become orange, the note is partially burn and you win a few points. 
	if the horizontal note bar become red, you don't have burn the note and you lose 50 points. 
	if the note go out of the screen without being burned, you lose 20 points.
